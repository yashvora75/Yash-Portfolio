NSE ⇄ BSE
Cash-to-Cash Cross-Exchange Arbitrage
NSE · BSE · Equities · Intraday
Detects real-time price discrepancies of the same equity listed on both NSE and BSE. The system simultaneously monitors bid/ask spreads on both exchanges tick-by-tick — when a spread exceeds the execution threshold, it fires simultaneous buy and sell orders on opposite legs to lock in a risk-free spread. Built on a low-latency event-driven architecture with broker API co-location awareness, order routing optimisation, and sub-second execution logic. Includes slippage modelling, brokerage cost netting, and dynamic spread filters to ensure only genuinely profitable opportunities are traded.

Exchange A ⇄ Exchange B
Crypto Cross-Exchange Price Arbitrage
Crypto · Multi-Exchange · Intraday
Monitors the same cryptocurrency pair simultaneously across two exchanges — flagging price divergences the moment they exceed the net cost of execution. The system connects to both exchange WebSocket feeds in parallel, computes real-time spread after fees, withdrawal costs, and transfer latency, and executes both legs atomically when the opportunity is confirmed profitable. Handles order book depth analysis to avoid partial fills, incorporates dynamic position sizing based on available liquidity, and logs every detected spread with full execution metadata for post-trade analysis and strategy refinement.
