Gamma Exposure (GEX)
Aggregates gamma weighted by open interest across every strike and expiry to map where dealer hedging pressure is heaviest. Positive GEX zones suppress intraday volatility as market makers buy dips and sell rallies to stay delta-neutral; negative GEX zones amplify directional moves as hedging flows align with price. Used in live trading to identify high-probability pinning levels near expiry, time entries around GEX flips, and avoid initiating positions inside unstable negative-gamma pockets.

VIX Range Prediction Model
Derives statistically expected daily high-low ranges directly from annualised implied volatility, scaling VIX to any intraday or overnight horizon. Calibrated against historical realised ranges to quantify the volatility risk premium — the persistent gap between what the market prices in and what actually occurs — giving a measurable edge when selling inflated premium. Feeds directly into strike selection, breakeven width calculation, and position sizing for live short-strangle and iron condor strategies running in the market.
